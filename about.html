OUR MISSION

Give every serious bettor access to the same analytics that professionals use.

The sportsbook industry spends billions modeling lines. Pinnacle exists to make the other side of that equation equally well-armed. We remove the vig, surface every +EV line, and flag every arb before the window closes.

Pinnacle does not accept wagers. We are an analytics-only platform — the Bloomberg Terminal of sports betting data.

THE STORY

Pinnacle started as an internal tool. Our founders were building sports models on the side — running no-vig calculators in Excel, manually comparing DraftKings to FanDuel in separate tabs, and logging CLV by hand in Notion.

The math was sound. The workflow was brutal.

We built the first version of the terminal for personal use. Then showed it to some friends who bet seriously. Then those friends started asking for access. After the twentieth request we realized: this should exist for everyone.

We’re a small, technical team. We don’t take outside capital. We don’t run ads. Pinnacle is funded by the people who use it.
